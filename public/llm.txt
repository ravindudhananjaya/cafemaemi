# Cafe Maemi - Authentic Indian & Nepalese Cuisine

## Overview
Cafe Maemi is a restaurant in Maebashi, Gunma, Japan, offering authentic Indian and Nepalese cuisine. We specialize in flavorful curries, freshly baked naan, and traditional dishes like Momos and Dal Bhat.

## Key Information
- **Location**: Maebashi, Gunma
- **Cuisine**: Indian, Nepalese
- **Highlights**: Butter Chicken Curry, Cheese Naan, Mutton Curry, Lunch Sets
- **Services**: Dine-in, Takeout, Lunch Drink Service (11:00-15:00)

## Menu Categories
- **Curry**: Butter Chicken, Mutton, Spinach Paneer, etc.
- **Naan/Rice**: Plain Naan, Cheese Naan, Garlic Naan, Biryani, Fried Rice
- **Sets**: Special Rice Set, Maemi Sets, Curry Sets
- **Noodles & MoMo**: Steam MoMo, Soup MoMo, ChowMein, Thukpa
- **Sides**: Tandoori Chicken, Samosa, Panipuri
- **Dessert**: Daily specials
- **Drinks**: Chai, Lassi, Coffee, Soft Drinks
