# Cafe Maemi - Full Website Content

## About Us
Cafe Maemi brings the authentic soul food of India and Nepal to Maebashi. Our chefs use a symphony of spices to create dishes that are both traditional and innovative. We pride ourselves on our warm hospitality and cozy, modern atmosphere.

## Menu Details

### Lunch Time Drink Service (11:00 - 15:00)
Free drink with lunch sets: Cola, Oolong Tea, Chai (Hot/Ice), Coffee (Hot/Ice).

### Curries
- **Butter Chicken Curry**: Rich tomato gravy with butter and cream. Mild spice.
- **Mutton Curry**: Tender mutton in spicy gravy. Spicy.
- **Spinach Paneer**: Spinach curry with cottage cheese. Mild.

### Naan & Rice
- **Cheese Naan**: Our most popular item! Naan stuffed with melting cheese.
- **Garlic Naan**: Topped with aromatic garlic.
- **Chicken Biryani**: Spiced aromatic rice with chicken.
- **Chicken Fried Rice**: Nepalese style fried rice.

### Sets
- **Special Rice Set**: The ultimate feast with Mutton Curry, Chicken Curry, Veg Curry, and sides.
- **Maemi Set A/B**: Choose your favorite curries with Tandoori Chicken, Salad, Naan/Rice, and Dessert.

### Noodles & MoMo
- **Steam MoMo**: Steamed Nepalese dumplings.
- **Soup MoMo**: Momos in warm, spiced soup.
- **Chilly MoMo**: Fried momos in spicy chilli sauce.
- **Chicken ChowMein**: Stir-fried noodles.
- **Chicken Thukpa**: Himalayan noodle soup.
