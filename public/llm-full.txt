# Cafe Maemi - Full Documentation

## Overview
Cafe Maemi is an authentic Indian and Nepalese restaurant located in Maebashi, Gunma, Japan. We pride ourselves on serving "soul food" from the Himalayas, prepared with a symphony of spices.
